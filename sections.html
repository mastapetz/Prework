Lions

Introduction

Lions are mainly found in Africa but can also be seen in India and parts of Asia. They like the hot places like the Sahara in Africa and the dryer regions.

Appearance

African lions are the lion you see most often. All lions look very similar but they've evolved to adapt to their home lands. The Asian lion is a good example. They have gold fur and a gold mane unlike the African lion. This is because of the rich dirt in Asia.
